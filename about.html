Our Story

Born from a passion for the great outdoors

Founded in 2008, Zipline Park started with a simple vision: to share the breathtaking beauty of our ancient forests with the world in a thrilling yet sustainable way.

What began as a small 3-zipline course has grown into one of the country's premier aerial adventure destinations. We've welcomed over 50,000 guests, from school children discovering nature to corporate teams building trust.

Green Adventure

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable

We believe that adventure shouldn't come at the cost of nature. Our park is built using "leave no trace" engineering techniques that protect the trees and wildlife.

Tree-Safe Hardware

Our compression-based mounting systems grow with the trees, avoiding permanent damage.

Zero-Waste Park

We recycle 100% of our waste and use solar energy to power our guest facilities.
