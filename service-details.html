Tour Overview

Experience the thrill of flying through the upper canopy of our pristine old-growth forest. Our signature Canopy Zipline Tour is an unforgettable adventure that combines high-speed thrills with breathtaking views.

The tour consists of 7 exhilarating ziplines, 2 suspended sky bridges, and a thrilling 50-foot rappel to finish. Accompanied by two highly trained guides, you'll learn about the local ecosystem while safely navigating the course.

Tour Highlights
