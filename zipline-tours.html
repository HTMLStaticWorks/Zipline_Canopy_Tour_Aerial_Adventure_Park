Stay Safe & Ready

Preparation & Requirements

Everything you need to know before you take flight.

Dress Code

Closed-toe shoes are mandatory. No skirts or loose jewelry. Long hair must be tied back.

Weight Limits

Minimum weight: 70 lbs. Maximum weight: 250 lbs. This ensures optimal safety and braking.

Safety Gear

We provide all equipment: full-body harnesses, helmets, and heavy-duty braking gloves.

Weather

We operate in rain or shine. Tours only stop for high winds, lightning, or extreme storms.
